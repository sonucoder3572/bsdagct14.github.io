About Baba Sahab Dr Ambedkar Gramin Charitable Trust

Get to know about our #bsdagct organization

On 20-08-2018, the inauguration ceremony of the office of Baba Sahab Dr. Ambedkar Rural Charitable Trust, Rampur Vijra Kohandaur Pratapgarh Uttar Pradesh (Reg.) Was completed by the Chief Guest in honor of Dr. R. A. Ratnakar (Deputy CMO Sultanpur).
Honorable Mahesh Buddhist and Dr. Subhash Gautam G Tathagat Buddha, lamp burnt and floral on the statue of Baba Sahab was done . Apart from this, Trust Founder Harikesh Kumar Gautam, President Vijay Sharoz, Treasurer Dinesh Gautam, General Secretary Avdesh Kumar Gautam, and all the other office bearers were present. Social worker Mohd Abdul Salam alias Latif was also present. The founder, Harikesh Kumar Gautam, congratulated and thanked all the greats which came on this auspicious occasion and thanked the Chief Guest and the greatness that came with them. And conveyed that all the people, united and motivated to create awareness in the interest of the society for education, security, health, environment and cleanliness, and motivated the poor to help in every way.

  • Providing free coaching facility to poor children.

  • Working to keep the natural environment clean.

  • Make all efforts to promote education.

  • Providing employment opportunities for unemployed and needy.
